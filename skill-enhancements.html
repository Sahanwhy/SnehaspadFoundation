WHO WE ARE

Transforming lives through Snehaspad Kaushal

Our rural-focused initiative is dedicated to narrowing the gap between potential and opportunity. We believe that skill development is the single most powerful tool for self-reliance.

  • ✦ Reducing rural unemployment
  • ✦ Driving women's financial independence
  • ✦ Encouraging local entrepreneurship
OUR CATALOGUE

Employment Oriented Modules

Tailoring & Fashion

Mastering the art of garment construction and modern fashion design to start home-based businesses.

Professional Baking

Comprehensive training in cake decoration, baking techniques, and running a micro-bakery.

Beauty & Grooming

Professional beautician services, salon management, and personal grooming certifications.

Digital Literacy

Basic to advanced computer education, essential office tools, and internet skills for the digital age.

Mushroom Farming

Scientific cultivation methods for high-yield mushroom production and market linkages.

🧼

Product Manufacturing

Creating daily-use household products like soap, detergent, and handwash for commercial sale.
