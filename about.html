🔹 OUR STORY

SNEHASPAD FOUNDATION was established on 27th July 2025 with a vision to create meaningful change in society. It was founded with the belief that true development begins at the grassroots level.

Seeing the challenges faced by rural youth, women, and economically weaker communities in the North Eastern Region, the foundation was formed to bridge the gap between opportunity and accessibility.

The organization began its journey with small awareness programs and community engagement initiatives and is steadily expanding towards skill development and employment generation projects across the region.

🎯

Our Mission

At SNEHASPAD FOUNDATION, our mission is to serve with compassion, empower with knowledge, and transform lives with opportunity.

We are committed to uplifting underprivileged and rural communities across the North Eastern Region of India by providing skill development, entrepreneurship support, educational assistance, and social awareness initiatives.

Through structured training programs, community engagement, and collaborative partnerships, we strive to build self-confidence, financial independence, and sustainable livelihoods among youth and women.

Our mission is not just to offer help — but to create long-term impact by enabling individuals to become self-reliant contributors to society.

🔭

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a progressive, self-reliant, and empowered society where every individual — regardless of background — has equal access to opportunity, dignity, and growth.

We envision a North East India where rural youth are skilled, women are economically independent, communities are socially aware, and development is sustainable and inclusive.

By aligning our work with the core values of Service, Empowerment, and Transformation, we aspire to become a catalyst for positive social change and community-driven development.
